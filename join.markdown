# Thank you for choosing to support TVGS as a member or with a donation!

As a member-supported nonprofit and a registered charity, we rely on our community of paid members and donors to help us further our mission. Your contributions play an essential role in supporting our ongoing efforts to foster a more diverse, approachable, and thriving game developer community in the Capital Region.

# Membership Tiers

Tech Valley Game Space offers two different membership options: standard member or coworking member.

All standard members shape the future of TVGS by voting in our board and coordinator elections. In addition to providing voting rights, standard memberships also come with a number of additional perks that are outlined on the <a target=null href="https://www.patreon.com/TechValleyGameSpace">TVGS Patreon page</a>.


<img src="/Images/community4-1024x576.jpg" alt="TVGS community event" />

Coworking members receive all of the same perks as standard members, as well as access to our shared studio space located at 30 3rd Street in Troy, NY. This community space is a vibrant creative hub that is specifically tailored to the needs of game developers and interactive artists.

Our communal game development studio is fully equipped with a wide variety of hardware and software including VR headsets, Wacom tablets, MIDI keyboards, iPads and more. For all of your “research” needs, we also provide an extensive library of games and consoles ranging from the retro era to modern day.

For more details about coworking at TVGS, including info about how to schedule a tour <a href="/contact">Contact Us</a>.


# Donation options
We’re extremely grateful for any donations! Your contributions enable TVGS to better support local independent game developers, and help us ensure that our community-building events and mentorship programs remain accessible to a diverse range of aspiring creators throughout the Capital Region.

<img src="/Images/social2.webp" alt="TVGS members posing for photo" />

To make a one-time donation, consider donating via PayPal.

If you’d like to support TVGS on a recurring monthly basis, you can do this via our <a target=null href="https://www.patreon.com/TechValleyGameSpace">Patreon</a> page – even if you’re not ready to become a member yet!

## Contribute when you shop

Humble Bundle’s Choose Your Own Charity (CYOC) option is also available on some bundles, as well as purchases made on the Humble Store. Look for the option to select a new charity from Humble Bundle’s database, and search for Tech Valley Game Space!
