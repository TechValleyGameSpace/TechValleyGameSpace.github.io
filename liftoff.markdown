<b>Lift Off 2022</b> is a <i>free</i> six-week program promoting diversity of gender and gender identity in game development. This virtual workshop invites nonbinary and trans people of all genders, and cis women, to create their own game with direct, one-on-one guidance from experienced mentors. Participants will practice applying essential game making skills including design, art, coding, audio composition, and more. No prior experience needed!

Have you ever wanted to make a game, but felt uncertain about how to get started? You’re not alone. Lift Off is designed for people just like you, because the truth is that you absolutely can make a game! Join our 2022 cohort and take the first step toward bringing your creative ideas to life with support from our team of experienced mentors and the friendly community at Tech Valley Game Space.
